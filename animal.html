Lion

The lion is a large carnivorous mammal of the Felidae family. It is often referred to as the "king of the jungle".

Elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal. It is known for its long trunk and large tusks.

Giraffe

The giraffe is the tallest living terrestrial animal. It is known for its long neck and distinctive coat patterns.

Horse

The horse (Equus ferus caballus)[2][3] is a domesticated, one-toed, hoofed mammal. It belongs to the taxonomic family Equidae and is one of two extant subspecies of Equus ferus.
